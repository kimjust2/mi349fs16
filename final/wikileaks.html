WikiLeaks

WikiLeaks is a self described “not-for-profit media organization.” On their website, they say their “goal is to bring important news and information to the public.” The organization came into existence in 2007. WikiLeaks helps facilitate journalists and whistleblower in leaking classified information and documents by providing an extremely secure dropbox. This is an organization dedicated to freedom of speech.
